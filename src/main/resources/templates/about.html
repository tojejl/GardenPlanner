Welcome!

This database will hold the twelve months of the year and about 24 different types of vegetables and herbs that are planted in a typical household garden. My stretch goal is to allow users to add other vegetable/herbs to the database. The goal of the site is to allow users the ability to search either by plant type or month to determine when is the best time to start planting.



Users can add plants to the database.
Users can edits information about each plant.
Users can search by keyword.
